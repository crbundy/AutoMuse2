Good night, Mr. Carraway. See you anon.”  “Of course you will,” confirmed Daisy. “In fact I think I’ll arrange a marriage. Come over often, Nick, and I’ll sort of—oh—fling you together. Besides, Nick’s going to look after her, aren’t you, Nick? She’s going to spend lots of weekends out here this summer.
