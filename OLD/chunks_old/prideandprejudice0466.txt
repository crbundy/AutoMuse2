This is your final resolve! Very       well. I shall now know how to act. Do not imagine, Miss Bennet,       that your ambition will ever be gratified. I came to try you. I       hoped to find you reasonable; but, depend upon it, I will carry       my point.”        In this manner Lady Catherine talked on, till they were at the       door of the carriage, when, turning hastily round, she added, “I       take no leave of you, Miss Bennet. I send no compliments to your       mother. You deserve no such attention. I am most seriously       displeased.”        Elizabeth made no answer; and without attempting to persuade her       ladyship to return into the house, walked quietly into it       herself. She heard the carriage drive away as she proceeded up       stairs.
