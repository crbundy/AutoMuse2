Dearest Lizzy, I hardly know what I would       write, but I have bad news for you, and it cannot be delayed.       Imprudent as the marriage between Mr. Wickham and our poor Lydia       would be, we are now anxious to be assured it has taken place,       for there is but too much reason to fear they are not gone to       Scotland. Colonel Forster came yesterday, having left Brighton       the day before, not many hours after the express.
