Mr. Denny addressed them directly, and entreated       permission to introduce his friend, Mr. Wickham, who had returned       with him the day before from town, and he was happy to say had       accepted a commission in their corps. This was exactly as it       should be; for the young man wanted only regimentals to make him       completely charming. His appearance was greatly in his favour; he       had all the best part of beauty, a fine countenance, a good       figure, and very pleasing address.
