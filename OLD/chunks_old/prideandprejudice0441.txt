On entering the room, he seemed to hesitate; but Jane       happened to look round, and happened to smile: it was decided. He       placed himself by her.        Elizabeth, with a triumphant sensation, looked towards his       friend. He bore it with noble indifference, and she would have       imagined that Bingley had received his sanction to be happy, had       she not seen his eyes likewise turned towards Mr. Darcy, with an       expression of half-laughing alarm.
