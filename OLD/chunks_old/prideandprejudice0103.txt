He was most       highly esteemed by Mr. Darcy, a most intimate, confidential       friend. Mr. Darcy often acknowledged himself to be under the       greatest obligations to my father’s active superintendence, and       when, immediately before my father’s death, Mr. Darcy gave him a       voluntary promise of providing for me, I am convinced that he       felt it to be as much a debt of gratitude to _him_, as of his       affection to myself.”        “How strange!” cried Elizabeth. “How abominable!
