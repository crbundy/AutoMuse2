Outline: A young scientist, Victor Frankenstein, becomes obsessed with creating life. He eventually succeeds in creating a creature, but when he sees what he has done, he flees in horror. The creature is left alone and misunderstood. He eventually turns to evil after being continually rejected by humans. The creature seeks revenge on his creator by destroying everything Frankenstein holds dear. In the end, Frankenstein destroys the creature he created, but not before the creature destroys him.
